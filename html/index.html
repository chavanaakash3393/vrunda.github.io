About Amit Engineers

Welcome to Amit Engineers & Tools Pvt. Ltd., delivering precision engineering solutions with quality, innovation & excellence.

Amit Engineers & Tools Pvt. Ltd. (AEATPL) was incorporated in 2014 in Chakan, Pune, one of Maharashtra's leading industrial hubs. Our sister concern, Amit Engineers, was established in 1995 and has earned Gold, Silver, and Bronze Quality Awards from Bajaj Auto Ltd.

  • ISO 9001:2015 TÜV
    Rheinland Certified
  • Trusted Partner for
    Automotive OEMs

Our mission is to deliver world-class sheet metal and fabrication solutions through advanced manufacturing, skilled professionals, and continuous quality improvement. We strive to exceed customer expectations with reliable products, timely delivery, and innovative engineering solutions.

To become a globally recognized engineering and manufacturing company by investing in advanced technology, expanding international partnerships, and delivering innovative solutions for the Automotive, EV, Electrical, and Industrial sectors.

Work Process

How We Complete Your Project

01
Design & Tool Development

Our engineering team studies customer drawings, develops tooling, dies, and manufacturing plans to ensure efficient production.

02
Sheet Metal Pressing & Fabrication

High-precision sheet metal components are manufactured using advanced hydraulic, pneumatic, and mechanical power presses.

03
Welding & Quality Inspection

Components undergo MIG, TIG, Spot, and Projection Welding followed by rigorous inspection using precision gauges and measuring instruments.

04
Assembly & Final Dispatch

Finished components are assembled, thoroughly inspected, securely packed, and delivered to OEM customers on schedule.
